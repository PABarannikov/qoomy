Delete Your Qoomy Account

Warning: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be removed.

What will be deleted:

  • Your account and profile information
  • All quiz rooms you created
  • All questions and images you uploaded
  • Your game history and scores

Request Account Deletion

To delete your account, please send an email to:

qoomy.app@gmail.com

Include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 days and send you a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

Alternative: Delete via Google Account

If you signed in with Google, you can also revoke Qoomy's access from your Google Account settings:

  1. Go to Google Account Permissions
  2. Find "Qoomy" in the list
  3. Click "Remove Access"

Note: This revokes access but doesn't delete stored data. Contact us to fully delete your data.
