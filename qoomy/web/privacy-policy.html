Qoomy Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

Introduction

Qoomy ("we", "our", or "the app") is a real-time multiplayer quiz game. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and web service.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve our service:

  • Account information: Email address and display name, provided during registration or obtained from your Google or Apple account when you sign in.
  • User ID: A unique identifier assigned to your account by Firebase Authentication.
  • Game data: Questions you create, answers you submit, team memberships, and game statistics.
  • Crash and performance data: We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and diagnostic information to improve app stability. This data includes device type, operating system version, and app state at the time of a crash.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To enable real-time multiplayer quiz gameplay
  • To display your profile and statistics to you and your team members
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues
  • To send push notifications about game activity (if you opt in)

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Storage). Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, cloud storage, crash reporting, and push notifications.
  • Google Sign-In: Optional sign-in method.
  • Apple Sign-In: Optional sign-in method on iOS.

Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

Qoomy is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@qoomy.online
